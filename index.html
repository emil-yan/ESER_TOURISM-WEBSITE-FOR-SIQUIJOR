Old Enchanted Balete

8 Rating

$200

Estimated to be over 400 years old, this massive tree is shrouded in local legend and believed by some to be inhabited by spirits. Visitors often stop here for a natural "fish spa" in the spring at its base.

2 days 3 nights

Lazi, Siquijor Discover

Paliton Beach

8 Rating

$200

Often called "Little Boracay," this beach is celebrated for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear water. It is widely considered the best spot on the island to watch the sunset.

San Juan, Siquijor Discover

Salagdoong Beach

8 Rating

$200

A favorite for thrill-seekers, this beach features famous cliff-diving platforms built into rock formations, with jumps as high as 35 feet. It is also known for its unique "glassy" turquoise water.

Maria, Siquijor Discover

Lazi Church & Convent

8 Rating

$200

Built in 1884, this UNESCO-worthy Spanish-era church is made of coral stone and hardwood. The convent across the street is one of the largest in Asia and houses the Siquijor Heritage Museum.
